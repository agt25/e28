Stonks 2009

Present-day winner or loser?

Find out how much you'd be worth today had you invested $10,000...
in a mystery stock back in 2009!
Win the game when you get 5 wins!
Lose the game with 5 losses :(
How to Play
1. Toggle the button of a mystery stock of your choice.
2. Click the 'Invest 10,000' button to go through with the purchase.
3. Ta-da! You'll know just how much (or little!) you won or lost!
4. Play again! The mystery stocks shuffle after every play!
